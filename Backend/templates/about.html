About CardioCare AI



CardioCare AI is an AI-powered Heart Disease Prediction System developed to help identify the risk of heart disease using Machine Learning. The system analyzes important health parameters such as age, gender, blood pressure, cholesterol level, heart rate, chest pain type, and other medical factors.

This project is designed to provide quick and accurate predictions, helping users understand their heart health status. By entering patient details, the system processes the information through a trained Machine Learning model and predicts whether there is a risk of heart disease.

CardioCare AI was developed using Python, Flask, HTML, CSS, and Machine Learning technologies. The objective of this project is to demonstrate how Artificial Intelligence can assist in healthcare and support early detection of heart-related problems.
